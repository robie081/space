Where nature comes first is not only a motto in Slovenia but the way it trully feels like.

Slovenian people found a way of protecting, enhancing and living in harmony with the natural world, in a sustainable way.

It was a refreshing and inspiring revelation.

Our first destination was Logar Valley.

Logar Valley

The Logar Valley is a flat green pasture surrounded by stunning rocky peaks with incredible waterfalls.

It is a great place for cycling and hiking.

The next leg of our jurney took us to lake Bled.

We visited the castle and its museum, enjoyed the views from above and walked the path circling the shores of the lake.

Emerald waters of lake Bled

Lake Bled is of glaciar origin and is crystal clear. You can take a boat to the lake's islet and visit the church.

I took the plunge and enjoyed a morning swim in this blue flag body of water.
